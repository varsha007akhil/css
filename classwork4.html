Annual Day Celebration 2026


Dear Students, Parents, and Staff,

We are pleased to announce that our school's Annual Day Celebration 2026 will be held on 15th August 2026 in the school auditorium. This special occasion is one of the most anticipated events of the academic year, celebrating the achievements, talents, and hard work of our students. The program will feature a variety of cultural performances, including dances, songs, dramas, speeches, and award presentations. Students who are participating are requested to attend all practice sessions regularly and be well-prepared for their performances. On the day of the event, all participants must report to the school by 8:00 AM in their costumes or school uniforms, as instructed by their teachers. Parents and guardians are warmly invited to attend the celebration and encourage the students with their presence. The event will begin at 9:30 AM and is expected to conclude by 3:30 PM. We kindly request everyone to arrive on time, maintain discipline throughout the program, and cooperate with the organizing committee to ensure the smooth conduct of the celebration. Let us come together to make this Annual Day a joyful, memorable, and successful event for our entire school community.

Date: 15th August 2026

Time: 9:30 AM-3:30 PM

Venue: School Auditorium
