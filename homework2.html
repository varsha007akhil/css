Mahindra Thar

price :Rs. 9.99 - 17.62 Lakh

The Mahindra Thar is for those who want a tough and capable off-roader that is perfect for your adventures. Now in its second-generation, the Thar boasts a thoroughly likeable design along with all the required features for your daily usage. Mahindra offers the Thar with turbo-petrol and diesel engines along with 4X4 and 4X2 options, the latter for buyers who use it on the road. It might not be the most practical or comfortable option, but its road presence and aura is practically unmatched at this price point.
